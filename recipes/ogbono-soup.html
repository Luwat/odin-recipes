Ingredients

  • Assorted meat and fish (beef, fish, stockfish etc.)
  • Ogbono Seeds
  • Palm Oil
  • Vegetable (spinach, pumpkin, bitter leaf)
  • Crayfish
  • Pepper and Onion
  • Salt and Seasoning Cubes

Steps

  1. Grind the Ogbono
  2. Cut your vegetables
  3. Cook the assorted meat with seasoning cubes
  4. Grind pepper and crayfish
  5. Boil some water and set aside
  6. Pour your desired oil and allow to stay for at least 40 minutes
  7. Pour your grinded Ogbono with crayfish and pepper
  8. Pour your already boiled water to dissolve the already fried ogbono
  9. Add your meat and allow for 10 minutes
  10. Add Your and seasoning cubes and salt to taste and allow for another 10 minutes
