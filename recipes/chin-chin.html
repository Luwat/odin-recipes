Ingredients

  • Flour - 1 paint
  • Nutmeg - 1 medium size
  • Liquid Milk - 2 tins
  • Milk Flavour - 2 sachets
  • Baking Powder - 2 tsp
  • Margarine - 500g
  • Sugar - 3 cups (More or less)
  • Eggs - 6
  • Water
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Rub in the margarine into the dry ingredients until it looks like bread crumbs
  3. Pour in the wet ingredients in bit and mix into a soft dough
  4. Roll out on a smooth flat surface using a rolling pin
  5. Cut into desired shapes and sizes
  6. Fry in bits until golden brown strain and allow to cool
